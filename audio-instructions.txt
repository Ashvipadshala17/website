# AUDIO FILES NEEDED

Place these audio files in the /public folder:

## 1. happy-birthday.mp3 (REQUIRED)
- Main birthday song that plays throughout the website
- Recommended: Traditional "Happy Birthday to You" song
- Duration: 30-60 seconds (will loop)
- File size: Keep under 5MB

## 2. celebration.mp3 (OPTIONAL)
- Sound effect when blowing candles
- Short celebration sound (1-3 seconds)
- Examples: cheer, applause, party horn

## FREE SOURCES:

### YouTube Audio Library
https://www.youtube.com/audiolibrary
- Search for "Happy Birthday"
- Download free, royalty-free versions

### Free Music Archive
https://freemusicarchive.org
- Filter by "Genre: Holiday"
- Look for birthday songs

### Pixabay Music
https://pixabay.com/music
- Search "birthday"
- Many free options available

### Alternative: Use Online Converters
1. Find a birthday song on YouTube
2. Use a YouTube to MP3 converter
3. Save as happy-birthday.mp3

## PLACEMENT:
After downloading, place files directly in the /public folder:
/public/happy-birthday.mp3
/public/celebration.mp3 (optional)

## TESTING:
Without these files, the website will still work but won't play music.
The music player will show but no audio will play.
